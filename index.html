Jorge Martinez-Gil

Senior Research Scientist in Artificial Intelligence & Data Engineering

Dr. Martinez-Gil holds a Ph.D. from the University of Malaga (2010) and brings over 15 years of research experience across Austria, Germany, and Spain. At the Software Competence Center Hagenberg, he leads projects on semantic technologies, machine learning pipelines, and FAIR data principles.

He has authored 70+ peer-reviewed publications and serves as Associate Editor for Expert Systems with Applications. His work is recognized with a DAAD Honorific Scholarship and the Best Academic Performance award from the University of Extremadura.
