Professional Profile

Dr. Jorge Martinez-Gil is an experienced computer scientist specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Data Engineering, and Knowledge Technologies. With a Ph.D. from the University of Malaga (2010), his academic path has led to research and teaching appointments in Austria, Germany, and Spain.

Currently based at the Software Competence Center Hagenberg (Austria), he drives applied research initiatives across domains such as semantic technologies, machine learning pipelines, and FAIR data principles. His academic engagements include prior faculty roles and ongoing thesis supervision across European institutions.

Dr. Martinez-Gil has authored over 70 peer-reviewed publications in internationally recognized journals and conferences. He serves as Associate Editor for *Expert Systems with Applications* and contributes regularly as a reviewer and evaluator for scientific journals and funding agencies.

His achievements include a DAAD Honorific Scholarship and the Best Academic Performance award from the University of Extremadura. With a strong focus on reproducible science and open collaboration, he is dedicated to impactful, high-quality research.
